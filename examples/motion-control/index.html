Tah Motion Control

Tah is a Bluetooth Low Energy open source development platform compatible with the Arduino.

In this example, we are going to take a look at the Tah Motion Control app which let's you control your laptop or desktop from a distance.


Step 1

Connect the Tah board to the USB port and open the Arduino IDE. Download the Tah library and rename it to Tah after extracting.

Step 2

  • Add it to the Arduino IDE Sketch->Import Library->Add Library
  • Restart the Arduino IDE and open the following example File -> Examples -> Tah -> MotionControl
  • Select Arduino Leonardo from Tools -> Board. Also select the correct USB port

Step 3

  • Restart the Arduino IDE and open the following example File -> Examples -> Tah -> MotionControl
  • Select Arduino Leonardo from Tools -> Board. Also select the correct USB port
  • Compile and Upload

Step 4

  • Next, get the Tah Motion Control app from the GitHub repo
  • Enable Bluetooth, and then open the app

Step 5

  • Scan for the nearby Bluetooth devices and select 'Tah Motion Control'
  • Once the indicator at the bottom turns green, press the home button which is in the top right corner of the screen
  • Select your operating system on the next screen.

Step 6

  • You can either control the Music Player(iTunes in the case of Mac) or a Presentation
  • When you are controlling the Music player, you Play, Pause, go to the Previous and Next song or control the volume. To control the volume, press the sound icon, and tilt your phone on the left and the right

Step 7

  • When you are controlling the Presentation, swipe on the screen in the direction of your next slide
