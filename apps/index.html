Apps

A lot of interesting things can happen when the Tah talks with your Bluetooth 4.0 enabled smartphone. Following is a list of apps that show some of the possibilities

Tah

The Tah app lets you control Tah's GPIO pins using our ArdSCL protocol in digital, analog and PWM modes. This app gives you the full power over GPIO acquisition by letting you read data from Tah's analog pins in real time.

  • iOS
  • Android
  • Source Code

Tah Play

The Tah Play app let you play games on your computer or play station using your smartphone. Just upload Play arduino code on Tah and plug it into computer or play station and you are ready to go. It's computer joystick comes with programmable keys which can be changed from game to game

  • iOS
  • Android
  • Source Code

Tah Motion

The Tah Motion app lets you control your PC or Mac with your phone's motion gestures. Swipe between desktops, control iTunes, VLC or Quick Time Player and use it as a controller to advance your Powerpoint or Keynote presentations.

  • iOS
  • Android
  • Source Code

Tah Sensor

The Tah Sensor app make it easier than ever to collect sensor data wirelessly on your smart device. Just attach a sensor to Tah and open Tah sensor app in your smartphone, connect to Tah and select sensor and pin number you have interfaced and thats all, Tah sensor app will automatically start fetching sensor data.

  • iOS
  • Android
  • Source Code

Tah RGB

The Tah RGB app helps you make a mood lamp and change the lighting on a bunch of RGB LEDs. You can use your phone to have the LEDs light up according to the colours of your choice or cycle through the selected colours in a rainbow mode.

  • iOS
  • Android
  • Source Code
